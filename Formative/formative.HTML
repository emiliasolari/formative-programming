MAKEUP GUIDE

The goal of this website is to give a brief introduction to basic makeup products and their function

FOUNDATION

Evens out skin tone making it look smoother and healthier

BLUSH

Used for colouring cheeks different shades of pink, orange or red

MASCARA

Used for tinting, lifting and giving volume to lashes

EYELINER

Used to define the eyes it is applied around the contours of the eye

PRIMER

Used before makeup to help it stay on better for longer

SETTING POWDER

Extend makeup longevity and mattify face. Usually used when having a oily face

LIPSTICK

Applies coloration and texture to lips

CONCEALER

Conceal eye bags, spots or blemishes. Similar to foundation but generally thicker

EYESHADOW

Placed on eyelids to create a variety of looks, it enhances the eyes
